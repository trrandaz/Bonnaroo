How To Get To Bonnaroo

If you’re coming from the east, use I-75 or I-59 to Chattanooga. From Chattanooga take I-24 west. Use Exit 111 towards HWY 55, turn right on HWY 55 and right again on Ragsdale Rd, one last right rurn onto New Bushy Branch and follow it to the The Bonnaroo Farm entrance.

If you’re coming from the west, use I-40, I-24, or I-65 to Nashville. From Nashville take I-24 east. Use Exit 111B to merge onto HWY 55 Northbound. Turn right onto Ragsdale Rd and right again onto New Bushy Branch and follow it to the The Bonnaroo Farm entrance.

Passenger Drop Off and Pick Up Location

Our official passenger drop off and pick up location is: The Bonnaroo Farm - 1560 New Bushy Branch, Manchester, Tennessee 37355
