What is Bonnaroo?

Set on the 700+ acres of Great Stage Park in Manchester, TN, Bonnaroo is a four-day festival that draws 80,000 fans every summer. Featuring over 150 musical performances, along with comedy, cinema, sustainability workshops and more, the grounds are converted into a virtual city of music and art.

Music and Entertainment

Bonnaroo features a diverse lineup of 150+ musicians and more every year. With 10+ performance stages, 4 campground party barns, and entertainment that goes ALL NIGHT LONG, you will NOT be bored.

Art and Creativity

The word “Bonnaroo” is actually Creole slang for “Good Stuff” and boy, do we have lots of that. Take a walk around the grounds, enjoy our numerous interactive art installations, and take home some cool crafts and posters from the marketplace or Bonnaroo Works Fund Auction while you’re at it!

Wonderful Foods

From mouth watering fish tacos and grass-fed beef burgers to the best damn doughnuts you've ever had (seriously), there are all sorts of food adventures throughout the festival. With plenty of vegan, vegetarian and gluten free options you WON'T be going hungry.

So Many Activites

So much room for activities! Whether you’re chilling out at yoga, painting water-colors with a local artist, or bringin’ the heat in the Eighth “First Annual” 5k, we’ve got what you’re looking for (and plenty of the unexpected too).
