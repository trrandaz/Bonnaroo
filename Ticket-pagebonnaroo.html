Tickets

You will need a festival ticket to enter. All The general tickets are four-day passes and the add-ons are dinner packages at the festival only.

4-Day Tickets

General Admission

  • 150+ performances on 10+ stages over four days - all night and all day
  • Access to the entire GA campground (Note: Every vehicle requires a car camping or day parking pass)
  • Food from 150+ vendors including vegan, vegetarian and gluten-free options
  • Bars, concessions, official band and festival merchandise, free water stations, and more amenities throughout the park and campground

All tiers are the same type of ticket. Prices subject to increase without notice.

General Admission+

Includes all of the perks of General Admission plus:

  • Fast-track GA+ entrance lane at both gates into Centeroo
  • Private Centeroo GA+ Lounge featuring premium restrooms, shade, fans, and more
  • Private, full-service bar in Centeroo lounge with beer, seltzers, wine, and cocktails for purchase plus complimentary water and soft drinks
  • Food for purchase from some of Bonnaroo's favorite food vendors inside the Centeroo GA+ Lounge

VIP

Includes all of the perks of General Admission plus:

  • Exclusive VIP Campground Plaza featuring air-conditioning, full-service bar, WiFi, daily yoga classes, games, live video feeds of the festival performances AND sports games, free hair styling, glitter, massages, and much more
  • Exclusive Centeroo VIP lounge with premium restrooms, games, private bar, photo booth, and more
  • VIP viewing areas for the What and Which stages and exclusive VIP entrance into Centeroo from the VIP Campground
  • Complimentary shower and premium restroom facilities with attendants (more facilities added for 2022!), mobile charging services, 24-hour info booth, and a commemorative Bonnaroo merchandise package

All vehicles must have a VIP Car or VIP RV pass to enter the VIP campground.

All tiers are the same type of ticket. Prices subject to increase without notice.
